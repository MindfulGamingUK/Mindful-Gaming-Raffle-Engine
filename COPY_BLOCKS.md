# Standard Copy Blocks & Disclaimers

Use these exact strings to ensure compliance and consistent branding across the SPA.

## Compliance Disclaimers

### D1: Non-Donation Notice
> **"Legal Notice:** This purchase is an entry into a promotion (Lottery or Prize Competition). It is not a charitable donation and is not Gift Aid eligible."

### D2: Residency & Age
> "Must be 18+ and a resident of Great Britain to enter. Proof of age and residency may be required to claim a prize."

### D3: Responsible Play
> "Please play responsibly. If you are concerned about your gambling, visit our [Support Page](/support) for help and self-exclusion tools."
