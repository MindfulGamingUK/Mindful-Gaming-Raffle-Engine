# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Mindful Gaming UK** | info@mindfulgaminguk.org

---

## Standard Position: No Refunds

Ticket purchases are non-refundable once a draw has been entered. This is consistent with standard lottery and competition law (Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, Reg. 28 — digital content exemption).

## Draw Cancellation by Mindful Gaming UK

If we cancel a draw before it takes place:

- All ticket holders will be refunded in full within **10 working days**
- Refunds are returned to the original payment method
- We will notify all entrants by email with the reason for cancellation

Reasons we may cancel:
- Prize unavailability (e.g. manufacturer supply issue)
- Minimum ticket threshold not reached within 30 days of draw date
- Regulatory requirement

## Draw Postponement

If a draw is postponed (not cancelled), ticket purchases remain valid for the rescheduled draw date. Entrants who do not wish to participate in the rescheduled draw may request a refund within **7 days** of the postponement announcement.

## Technical Errors

If a payment is taken but no entry is recorded due to a technical error, contact us at info@mindfulgaminguk.org within 14 days with your payment reference. We will investigate and either confirm your entry or issue a full refund within 5 working days.

## Duplicate Payments

If you are charged more than once for the same entry due to a technical fault, contact us immediately. We will refund any duplicate charge within 5 working days.

## How to Request a Refund

Email: info@mindfulgaminguk.org
Subject: "Refund Request — [Draw Name]"
Include: your name, email address used at entry, payment reference, and reason.
