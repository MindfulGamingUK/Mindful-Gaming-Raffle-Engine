## What Data We Collect

When you enter a draw or competition we collect:

- Name (first name, last name)
- Email address
- Date of birth (to verify 18+ eligibility)
- Residency confirmation (GB-only checkbox)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe/PayPal — we do not store card details)
- Entry history (ticket numbers, draw reference, amount paid)
- Marketing consent preference (opt-in only, unchecked by default)

## Lawful Basis

| Purpose | Lawful Basis |
|---|---|
| Processing your entry and payment | Contract (Article 6(1)(b) UK GDPR) |
| Age and eligibility verification | Legal obligation (Gambling Act 2005) |
| Regulatory returns (LAA5) filed with Birmingham City Council | Legal obligation |
| Winner notification and prize fulfilment | Contract |
| Marketing emails (draw reminders, charity updates) | Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — only where explicitly opted in |

## How Long We Keep It

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Entry records, payments | 7 years (financial records requirement) |
| Winner records | 7 years |
| Marketing consent records | Until withdrawn + 1 year |
| Age/eligibility verification | Duration of account + 1 year |

## Who We Share It With

- **Stripe / PayPal** — payment processing only (their own privacy policies apply)
- **Courier services** — name and delivery address for prize dispatch only
- **Birmingham City Council** — aggregated draw returns (no individual data)
- We do not sell or share personal data with any marketing third parties.

## Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request erasure (subject to legal retention obligations), restrict processing, data portability, and to withdraw marketing consent at any time.

To exercise any right: email info@mindfulgaminguk.org with the subject "Data Rights Request."

## Cookies

The raffle engine uses session storage only (no tracking cookies). The Wix platform may set functional cookies — see Wix's privacy policy at wix.com/about/privacy.
