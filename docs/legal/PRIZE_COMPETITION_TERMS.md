# Prize Competition Terms & Conditions

**Mindful Gaming UK — Prize Competition (Skill)**
Charity No. 1212285 | Promoter: Mindful Gaming UK, 5 Longmoor Road, Sutton Coldfield, B73 6UB

---

## 1. Nature of the Competition

1.1 This is a Prize Competition requiring an element of skill (Gambling Act 2005, s.14). It is **not** a lottery or a free-entry prize draw.
1.2 Entry fees are charged to enter and answer a skill question. Entrants who answer incorrectly are excluded from the draw.
1.3 A free postal route to enter exists and will be stated on each competition page (see section 2.4).

## 2. Eligibility & Entry

2.1 Open to UK residents aged 18 or over.
2.2 Employees, trustees, and immediate family members of Mindful Gaming UK are not eligible.
2.3 Each entry requires payment of the stated entry fee and a correct answer to the skill question.
2.4 **Free entry route:** Entrants may enter without payment by posting their name, address, date of birth, competition name, and answer to: Mindful Gaming UK, 5 Longmoor Road, Sutton Coldfield, B73 6UB. One free postal entry per household per competition.
2.5 Entry constitutes acceptance of these terms in full.

## 3. Skill Question

3.1 The skill question is presented during the entry process. The correct answer is verifiable by reasonable care or research.
3.2 Entrants must select one answer from the options provided. Only entries with the correct answer are entered into the final draw.
3.3 The promoter's decision on the correct answer is final.
3.4 In the event of a tie (multiple correct entries), the winner is selected by random draw from all correct entries.

## 4. Draw & Winner Selection

4.1 The winning entry is selected by cryptographic RNG from all valid (correctly answered) entries.
4.2 The draw is conducted on or after the stated draw date.
4.3 Winners are notified by email within 5 working days.
4.4 Winners must respond within 28 days. Unclaimed prizes are forfeited.

## 5. Prizes, Refunds & Data

5.1 Prizes are as described on the competition page. No cash alternative unless stated.
5.2 Entry fees are non-refundable once submitted, except where the competition is cancelled by Mindful Gaming UK.
5.3 Personal data is handled in accordance with our Privacy Notice.

## 6. Advertising

6.1 All promotional material includes the 18+ age restriction and UK residency requirement.
6.2 Prize Competition entries are not donations and are not Gift Aid eligible.
