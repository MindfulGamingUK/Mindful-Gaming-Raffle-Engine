# Safer Play & Support

**Mindful Gaming UK** — We care about responsible participation

---

## Our Commitment

We run regulated draws to fund gaming disorder awareness. We take responsible play seriously and provide the following protections for all participants.

## Age Verification

All participants must confirm they are aged 18 or over at the point of entry. We reserve the right to request proof of age before dispatching a prize.

## Spending Limits

Your account settings allow you to set a **monthly spending limit** on draw entries. Once set, this cannot be increased for 24 hours (cooling-off period). We recommend setting a limit you are comfortable with before making your first entry.

## Self-Exclusion

You can self-exclude from all Mindful Gaming UK draws at any time:
- **In-app:** Profile → Safer Play → Self-Exclude
- **By email:** info@mindfulgaminguk.org — Subject: "Self-Exclusion Request"

Self-exclusion is effective immediately and lasts for a minimum of 6 months. We will not contact you with promotional draw reminders during this period. Self-exclusion does not affect any existing entries already paid for.

## Signs That Gambling May Be Becoming a Problem

- Spending more than you intended or can afford
- Feeling anxious, irritable, or secretive about your gambling
- Chasing losses
- Gambling to escape stress or problems
- Neglecting work, family, or social activities

If any of these apply to you or someone you know, please seek support.

## Note on Our Mission

Mindful Gaming UK exists to support people affected by gaming disorder. Our draws fund that mission. If our draws ever become a source of stress for you rather than fun, please stop and reach out. Your wellbeing matters more than a ticket sale.
