## 1. Eligibility

1.1 Participants must be aged 18 or over.
1.2 Participation is open to residents of Great Britain only (England, Scotland, Wales). Northern Ireland residents are excluded.
1.3 Employees, trustees, and immediate family members of Mindful Gaming UK are not permitted to enter.
1.4 Entry constitutes acceptance of these terms in full.

## 2. Entry

2.1 Each ticket represents a single chance in the draw. Tickets are priced as stated on the relevant draw page.
2.2 All tickets within a single draw are priced uniformly (Gambling Act 2005, s.258).
2.3 The maximum number of tickets available per draw is stated on the draw page. Once sold out the draw closes.
2.4 Entry is online only. No postal entries are accepted.
2.5 Tickets are non-refundable except where the draw is cancelled by Mindful Gaming UK (see section 6).
2.6 Entry is a purchase of a chance and is **not a donation**. It is **not eligible for Gift Aid**.

## 3. The Draw

3.1 Draws are conducted on or after the draw date stated on the draw page.
3.2 The winning ticket is selected by cryptographic random number generation (RNG). The algorithm and seed are logged to our Wix CMS audit trail.
3.3 The promoter may delay a draw if the minimum ticket threshold has not been reached. Participants will be notified.
3.4 The promoter reserves the right to cancel a draw and issue refunds if a minimum threshold cannot be met within 30 days of the stated draw date.

## 4. Winners

4.1 Winners are notified by email to the address provided at entry, within 5 working days of the draw.
4.2 Winners must respond within 28 days of notification to claim their prize. Unclaimed prizes are forfeited and retained for charitable purposes.
4.3 Winners may be required to provide proof of age and UK residency before a prize is dispatched.
4.4 Winner details (first name and county only) may be published with the winner's explicit written consent.

## 5. Prizes

5.1 Prizes are as described on the draw page at the time of entry.
5.2 No cash alternative is offered unless explicitly stated.
5.3 Prizes are dispatched to UK mainland addresses only. Additional shipping charges may apply for remote locations.
5.4 Mindful Gaming UK accepts no responsibility for prizes lost or damaged in transit once handed to the carrier.
5.5 Total prize value for any single draw shall not exceed £25,000 in accordance with Small Society Lottery regulations.

## 6. Cancellation & Refunds

6.1 Mindful Gaming UK reserves the right to cancel any draw. In the event of cancellation, all ticket holders will receive a full refund to the original payment method within 10 working days.
6.2 No refunds are issued once a draw has been conducted.

## 7. Proceeds

7.1 Net proceeds (after prize costs, platform fees, and legitimate expenses) are applied to the charitable purposes of Mindful Gaming UK — specifically gaming disorder awareness and support services.
7.2 A minimum of 20% of gross proceeds is applied to charitable purposes in every draw (Gambling Act 2005, s.258 requirement).
7.3 An annual return (LAA5) is filed with Birmingham City Council within 3 months of each draw.

## 8. Data & Privacy

8.1 Personal data collected at entry is processed in accordance with our Privacy Notice (docs/legal/PRIVACY_NOTICE_RAFFLES.md).
8.2 Data is not shared with third parties except payment processors (Stripe/PayPal) and prize fulfilment couriers.

## 9. Disputes

9.1 All decisions of Mindful Gaming UK regarding draw results are final.
9.2 Any disputes should be submitted in writing to info@mindfulgaminguk.org within 14 days of the draw date.
9.3 These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales.
